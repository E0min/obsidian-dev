# 심화 14-15번 전략

14번과 15번은 IH와 AL을 가르는 구간이다. 완벽한 사회 지식을 말하려고 하기보다, **구조가 있는 의견**을 말하는 것이 중요하다.

## 14번. 비교 및 대조

질문 유형:

- 과거와 현재 비교
- A와 B 비교
- 아이와 어른의 활동 차이
- 국내와 해외의 차이
- 예전 취향과 현재 취향 비교

## 비교 답변 공식

1. 차이가 있다고 선언
2. A 설명
3. B 설명
4. 개인 선호와 이유

템플릿:

> I think there are a few clear differences between [A] and [B]. First of all, [A] is more [feature], while [B] is more [feature]. Also, [A] usually involves [detail], but [B] tends to involve [detail]. Personally, I prefer [A/B] because it fits my lifestyle better.

## 과거와 현재 변화 공식

템플릿:

> In the past, people used to [past habit]. But these days, people are more likely to [current habit]. I think this change has happened because [reason]. Personally, I think it is mostly a positive change, but there are some downsides as well.

카페 변화:

> In the past, people went to cafes mainly to drink coffee and talk with friends. But these days, cafes have become more like multi-purpose spaces. People study, work, take photos, and even hold small meetings there. I think this change has happened because people want comfortable spaces outside their homes.

영화 변화:

> In the past, people had to go to a movie theater to watch new movies. But these days, many people watch movies on streaming platforms at home. It is much more convenient, but I still think theaters have a special atmosphere that streaming cannot replace.

여행 변화:

> In the past, people relied on travel agencies or guidebooks. But these days, people can plan everything with their smartphones. They can book hotels, check reviews, and find restaurants with just a few taps.

## 15번. 사회적 이슈 및 뉴스

질문 유형:

- 특정 주제와 관련된 문제
- 최근 뉴스
- 사람들이 걱정하는 점
- 기술 변화의 영향
- 사회적 트렌드

## 이슈 답변 공식

1. 가장 큰 이슈 하나 선택
2. 구체 예시
3. 장점 또는 단점
4. 내 의견
5. 균형 잡힌 결론

템플릿:

> In my opinion, one of the biggest issues related to [topic] is [issue]. For example, [specific example]. This can be a problem because [reason]. Of course, there are some benefits, but I think people need to find a good balance. Personally, I believe [opinion].

## 만능 이슈 1. 스마트폰과 편의성

사용 가능한 주제:

- 여행
- 카페
- 영화
- 음악
- 공원
- 음식점

답변:

> In my opinion, the biggest change these days is definitely because of smartphones. In the past, people had to check maps, call places, or ask other people for information. But now, everything can be done with just a few taps. For example, when people travel, they can book hotels, check restaurant reviews, and find directions instantly. It is incredibly convenient.  
>  
> But on the flip side, some people rely on their phones too much. Even when they go to beautiful places, they focus more on taking photos for social media than enjoying the actual moment. So, I think smartphones are useful, but people need to find a good balance.

## 만능 이슈 2. 비용 상승

사용 가능한 주제:

- 여행
- 카페
- 영화
- 외식
- 주거

답변:

> I would say one of the biggest issues these days is that everything has become more expensive. For example, traveling, eating out, and even going to a cafe cost much more than before. Because of this, people are more careful about how they spend their money.  
>  
> Personally, I think this has changed people's lifestyle. Instead of going far away, many people choose simple activities like walking in a park, cooking at home, or having a staycation. I think this is not always a bad thing because it helps people find small happiness in everyday life.

## 만능 이슈 3. 환경과 여행

사용 가능한 주제:

- 해변
- 여행
- 공원
- 야외 활동

답변:

> One issue that comes to mind is the environmental problem caused by tourism. Popular beaches and parks can become crowded, and sometimes people leave trash behind. This can damage the natural environment and make the place less enjoyable for everyone.  
>  
> I think travelers should be more responsible. For example, they should bring their trash back, avoid using too much plastic, and respect local rules. I still love traveling, but I believe people should enjoy nature without damaging it.

## 개인 의견 마무리 문장

- Personally, I think this is a positive change because it gives people more options.
- But at the same time, I think we need to be careful about the negative side.
- In the end, balance is the most important thing.
- I do not think there is one perfect answer, but people should be more responsible and thoughtful.
