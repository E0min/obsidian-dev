# 롤플레이 11-13번 전략

롤플레이는 완벽한 예측보다 구조가 중요하다. 상황이 달라도 다음 공식은 거의 그대로 쓴다.

## 전체 흐름

| 문항 | 유형 | 해야 할 일 |
|---:|---|---|
| 11 | 정보 요청 | 질문 3~4개 |
| 12 | 문제 해결 | 상황 설명 + 사과 + 대안 2개 |
| 13 | 유사 경험 | 비슷한 과거 경험 설명 |

## 11번. 정보 요청

목표:

- 전화하는 듯한 톤으로 시작
- 질문 3~4개
- 마지막에 감사 인사

기본 템플릿:

> Hi, I am calling because I would like to ask a few questions about [topic]. First, could you tell me [question 1]? Also, I was wondering if [question 2]. Another thing is, do I need to [question 3]? Lastly, how much does it cost, and is there anything I should bring? Thank you so much.

카페/식당 예약:

> Hi, I am calling because I would like to make a reservation. First, do you have a table available this Saturday evening? Also, how many people can sit at one table? Another thing is, do we need to pay a deposit in advance? Lastly, is parking available near the cafe?

여행/숙소:

> Hi, I am calling to ask a few questions about the room. First, is the room available this weekend? Also, how far is it from the beach? Another thing is, does the room have a kitchen? Lastly, what time is check-in?

## 12번. 문제 해결 및 대안 제시

12번은 배점이 높은 핵심 문항으로 본다.

반드시 포함:

1. 문제 설명
2. 사과
3. 대안 2개
4. 선택권 넘기기

기본 템플릿:

> Hey, Eva. It is me. Listen, I have some bad news. We were supposed to [original plan], but something unexpected came up, and I do not think the original plan will work. I am really sorry about this.  
>  
> So, I have two suggestions. First, we could [option A]. Or, if that does not work for you, we could [option B]. I will make it up to you. Let me know what you think. Call me back when you can.

날씨 문제:

> We were supposed to go to the park today, but it looks like it is going to rain heavily.

대안:

> First, we could go to a cozy cafe instead and talk there. Or, if you really want to walk outside, we could postpone it to next weekend.

예약 문제:

> I just found out that the cafe is fully booked, so we cannot get a table today.

대안:

> First, we could go to another cafe nearby. Or, if you prefer that original place, we could change the date and make a proper reservation.

물건 고장:

> I am really sorry, but I accidentally broke the MP3 player that I borrowed from you.

대안:

> First, I can pay for the repair. Or, if it cannot be fixed, I will buy you a new one.

## 13번. 유사 경험 말하기

13번은 더 이상 연기하지 않는다. 실제 경험처럼 차분히 말한다.

구조:

1. 비슷한 경험 도입
2. 언제, 어디서, 누구와
3. 문제 발생
4. 해결
5. 배운 점

기본 템플릿:

> Actually, something similar happened to me a few years ago. I was planning to [activity] with my friend, but suddenly [problem]. At first, I panicked a little because I did not expect that at all. But I tried to stay calm and think of another option. In the end, we [solution], and it turned out pretty well. Since then, I have learned that it is always better to have a backup plan.

예약 문제 유사 경험:

> Actually, something similar happened to me last year. I was planning to go to a popular cafe with my friend, but when we got there, the place was completely full. At first, we were disappointed, but we quickly searched for another cafe nearby. In the end, we found a quieter place, and it was even better than the original one. Since then, I have always tried to make a reservation in advance.

여행 문제 유사 경험:

> Actually, something similar happened during a trip to Jeju. My family and I planned to go to the beach, but it suddenly started raining. At first, I was disappointed, but we changed our plan and went to a small cafe near the ocean. After a while, the rain stopped, and we saw a beautiful sunset. It became one of my favorite travel memories.

## 롤플레이 만능 표현

- Something unexpected came up.
- I am really sorry about this.
- I have two suggestions.
- If that does not work for you,
- I will make it up to you.
- Let me know what you think.
- Call me back when you can.
- Since then, I have learned that having a backup plan is really important.
