# IH 고득점 문장장치

IH를 노릴 때는 어려운 단어를 많이 넣는 것보다, 문장과 문장 사이를 자연스럽게 연결하고 시제와 구조를 안정적으로 유지하는 것이 중요하다.

반드시 연습할 장치:

1. 필러
2. 관계사
3. 현재완료
4. would

## 1. 필러

필러는 침묵을 피하고 생각할 시간을 벌기 위한 장치다. 너무 많이 넣으면 산만해지므로 문장 시작과 전환 지점에만 넣는다.

### 시작할 때

- Well, to be honest,
- Actually,
- You know, Eva,
- When it comes to this topic,
- I would say that

예시:

> Well, to be honest, I am not a very sporty person.

> You know, Eva, I usually prefer quiet places.

### 생각할 때

- Let me think.
- Let me see.
- That is a good question.
- I have never really thought about that before.

예시:

> That is a good question. Let me think for a second.

### 정정할 때

- I mean,
- What I mean is,
- Actually, let me put it this way.

예시:

> I mean, it is not a serious workout. It is more like a mental healing session for me.

### 추가할 때

- On top of that,
- Another thing is that
- What is more,
- Plus,

예시:

> On top of that, the cafe plays soft music, which makes the atmosphere even better.

### 마무리할 때

- So, overall,
- That is why
- For these reasons,
- In the end,

예시:

> So, overall, walking in the park is one of the easiest ways for me to recharge.

## 2. 관계사 사용

관계사는 문장을 길고 자연스럽게 만들어준다. IH 답변에서는 짧은 문장만 나열하는 것보다, 관계사를 이용해 정보를 붙이는 연습이 필요하다.

### 장소 + where

> I like cafes where I can listen to calm music and focus on myself.

> The park where I usually walk has a small lake and a beautiful trail.

### 사람 + who

> I usually go there with my family, who also enjoy quiet outdoor places.

> My friend, who is a huge coffee lover, introduced me to that cafe.

### 사물/활동 + that

> I like music that helps me calm down after a stressful day.

> I prefer simple dishes that I can cook without too much pressure.

### 문장 전체 보충 + which

> The cafe plays soft jazz music, which makes the atmosphere really cozy.

> I walked for almost two hours, which was much longer than I expected.

## 3. 현재완료 사용

현재완료는 과거 경험이 현재까지 이어지는 느낌을 줄 때 좋다.

### have always liked

> I have always liked calm music because it helps me relax.

> I have always enjoyed beach trips because they make me feel free.

### have been into

> Recently, I have been into cooking simple pasta at home.

> I have been into walking in the evening because it helps me clear my head.

### have visited / have tried

> I have visited Jeju several times, but I never get tired of it.

> I have tried a few different cafes in my neighborhood, but this one is still my favorite.

### has become

> Walking in the park has become my favorite way to relieve stress.

> Listening to music while cooking has become a small routine for me.

## 4. would 사용

would는 가정, 선호, 반복 습관, 부드러운 의견을 말할 때 유용하다.

### 반복 습관

> When I was younger, I would watch movies almost every weekend.

> During my staycation, I would usually cook something simple and watch a movie at home.

### 선호

> I would rather go to a quiet beach than a crowded shopping mall.

> I would choose a cozy cafe over a noisy restaurant.

### 부드러운 의견

> I would say smartphones have changed the way people travel.

> I would say walking is not really exercise for me. It is more like a way to clear my mind.

### 가정

> If I had more time, I would travel to a quiet island again.

> If the weather were nice, I would definitely go for a walk in the park.

## 5. IH용 문장 확장 공식

짧은 문장:

> I like cafes.

IH용 확장:

> I like cafes where I can listen to calm music, sit by the window, and organize my thoughts.

짧은 문장:

> I went to Jeju.

IH용 확장:

> I have visited Jeju several times, and the trip that I remember the most was the one I took with my family last summer.

짧은 문장:

> I walk in the park.

IH용 확장:

> I would usually walk in the park near my apartment, which has a peaceful trail and a small lake.

## 6. 시험장에서 바로 쓰는 연결 문장

묘사에서 경험으로:

> Speaking of that place, I remember one time when I went there with my family.

일반 취향에서 이유로:

> The main reason I like it is that it helps me relax without spending too much money.

현재에서 과거로:

> These days, I usually do this alone, but a few years ago, I had a really memorable experience with my friend.

비교로 넘어갈 때:

> Compared to the past, I think people these days care more about convenience and personal time.
