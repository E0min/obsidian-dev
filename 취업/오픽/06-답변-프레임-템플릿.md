# 답변 프레임 템플릿

답변은 통암기보다 프레임을 먼저 외운다. 질문이 바뀌어도 프레임은 유지하고, 주제어만 바꾼다.

## 유형 1. 묘사 프레임

사용 상황:

- 장소 묘사
- 좋아하는 대상 설명
- 선호 이유

구조:

1. 대상 한 문장 소개
2. 특징 2~3개
3. 내가 좋아하는 이유
4. 감정으로 마무리

템플릿:

> Well, to be honest, one of my favorite places is [place]. It is a [adjective] place that has [feature 1], [feature 2], and [feature 3]. What I like most about it is the atmosphere. It feels [emotion], so whenever I go there, I can [benefit]. That is why I really enjoy spending time there.

예시:

> Well, to be honest, one of my favorite places is a small cafe near my apartment. It is a cozy place that has warm lighting, comfortable seats, and soft background music. What I like most about it is the atmosphere. It feels peaceful, so whenever I go there, I can slow down and organize my thoughts.

## 유형 2. 루틴/과정 프레임

사용 상황:

- 평소 활동
- 단계 설명
- 주말 루틴

구조:

1. 보통 언제 하는지
2. first, then, after that
3. 마지막 감정

템플릿:

> I usually [activity] when [situation]. First, I [step 1]. Then, I [step 2]. After that, I [step 3]. I do not try to make it perfect. I just enjoy the process because it helps me [benefit].

예시:

> I usually **take a walk** when **I want to clear my head**. First, I **put on my comfortable sneakers**. Then, I **head out to the park near my house**. After that, I **walk slowly while listening to my favorite music**. I just enjoy the process because it helps me **relieve stress and feel refreshed**.


## 유형 3. 최근/최초 경험 프레임

사용 상황:

- 최근에 한 일
- 처음 해본 경험
- 취향 변화

구조:

1. 언제, 어디서, 누구와
2. 무엇을 했는지
3. 좋았던 점
4. 현재 감정

템플릿:

> The last time I [activity] was [time]. I went to [place] with [person]. At first, I just wanted to [purpose], but it turned out to be much better than I expected. We [detail 1] and [detail 2]. What I remember most is [memorable detail]. After that, I felt [emotion].

예시:

> The last time I went to a park was two weeks ago. I went there with my family after dinner. At first, I just wanted to take a short walk, **but it turned out to be much better than I expected**. We walked around the lake and talked about small things. What I remember most is the cool evening breeze. After that, I felt completely refreshed.

## 유형 4. 기억에 남는 경험 프레임

사용 상황:

- memorable
- unforgettable
- special
- impressive

구조:

1. 기억에 남는 사건 선언
2. 배경
3. 예상 밖 문제나 감정
4. 해결/결과
5. 왜 기억에 남는지

템플릿:

> The most memorable experience I had with [topic] was [time]. I was with [person], and we went to [place]. Everything was going well at first, but suddenly [unexpected event]. I was a little [emotion], but we decided to [solution]. In the end, it became a funny and unforgettable memory. I still remember it because [reason].

예시:

> The most memorable trip I had was a beach trip to Jeju with my family. Everything was going well at first, but suddenly it started raining heavily. I was a little disappointed, but we decided to go to a small cafe near the beach and wait until the rain stopped. In the end, we saw an amazing sunset after the rain. I still remember it because the view was absolutely breathtaking.

## 답변 길이 조절

짧게 말해야 할 때:

- 묘사 2개 + 감정 1개만 말한다.

길게 말해야 할 때:

- 관계사 문장 1개
- 현재완료 문장 1개
- would 문장 1개
- 개인 감정 1개를 추가한다.

확장 예시:

> I have been going to this cafe for years, and it has become one of my favorite places in my neighborhood. If I had more free time, I would probably go there more often.
